About Us

The Kisan Sahayak portal is a digital platform designed to provide various services and information to farmers, primarily focusing on agricultural support and schemes. It acts as a common interface for accessing information related to government schemes, market prices, weather updates, and other relevant agricultural resources. Here's a more detailed look at the Kisan Sahayak portal: Key Features and Services:

Information Hub:

It serves as a central rpository for information on various government schemes, including those related to agriculture, horticulture, animal husbandry, and more.

Farmer Registration:

The portal facilitates farmer registration for various schemes and services, often through different channels like web portals, call centers, or SMS.

Access to Market Information:

Farmers can find details on market prices, Minimum Support Prices (MSP), and nearby markets.

Weather Updates:

The portal provides access to weather information, including forecasts for the current day and the next few days.

Crop Insurance:

Information and details about crop insurance schemes are available on the portal.

Soil Health:

Farmers can find information about Soil Health Cards and nearby soil testing laboratories.

Agricultural Machinery:

The portal provides information on agricultural machinery manufacturers, dealers, and related services.

Extension Services:

Information on government schemes, training programs, and extension services are also accessible.

mKisan Integration:

The portal is linked with the mKisan portal, which utilizes mobile messaging to reach farmers with relevant information. Specific Examples of Services:
